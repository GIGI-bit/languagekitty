How thermoses work?

Each thermos contains two containers inside of each other. The one on the outside is metal, the one is inside is mostly made of glass. There is no air between the two containers. It means there is empty airless space between two containers. Because of airless space heat transfer is not possible and heat exchange between the weather outside and inside is also not possible. Besides, those producers make inside and outside of thermos bright to keep warmth longer. Good thermos should keep warmth longer.
